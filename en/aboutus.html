ABOUT US

Steelinvest, s.r.o. was founded in 1998 as a company for trading non-ferrous metals. Over its existence, the company has established itself as a reliable supplier of aluminum, aluminum alloys, copper, brass, lead, zinc, and other non-ferrous metals.

With the aim of providing our customers with even better services and faster deliveries, we have expanded our capacity over the years to include a warehouse for reliable just-in-time deliveries to our European clients. This warehouse allows us to manage inventory more efficiently and respond more flexibly to market needs, thereby increasing customer satisfaction. We continuously strive to improve and keep up with the latest trends and technologies, which is why we regularly attend the latest conferences and professional events in our industry.

PARTNERS

Over our many years of experience in the industry, we have built strong and reliable relationships with partners around the world. Our global network of collaborators and suppliers enables us to provide the highest quality of services and products to our customers.

We are proud of our partnerships, which include renowned companies and organizations in Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and other regions. These strategic collaborations help us ensure continuous growth and innovation, allowing us and our partners to achieve common successes.

STANDARDS

We adhere to high standards and are proud to have obtained the ISO 9001:2015 certificate. In addition, we have implemented specific ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) standards and policies, along with KYS (Know Your Supplier) verification policies.

We regularly visit each of our suppliers in person to verify that they consistently meet our strict standards.
