CERTIFICATE

Our company is certified to meet international standards of quality and reliability. Steelinvest has earned the prestigious ISO 9001 certificate for trading, storage, and supply of non-ferrous metals.

This certificate demonstrates our commitment to consistently providing high-quality products and services, ensuring customer satisfaction, and continually improving our processes. Our compliance with ISO 9001 standards guarantees that our operations meet the highest levels of efficiency and quality.

Our company is also certified under the REACH regulation.

The REACH certification emphasizes our commitment to safety and health in the handling of chemical substances. This certification ensures that our products comply with the strictest European regulations, contributing to higher quality and safety of our products and protection of the environment.
